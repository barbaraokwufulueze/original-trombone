The trombone is a beautiful musical instrument in the brass family. As with all brass instruments, sound is produced when the player's vibrating lips cause the air column inside the instrument to vibrate. Unlike most brass instruments that use valves to alter the pitch, trombones have a telescoping slide mechanism that controls the pitch instead.
